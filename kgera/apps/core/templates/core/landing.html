CREATING A RESIDENT ACCOUNT

A Resident Account is user account for residents of the estate. The Account is to be active for the duration of the resident's stay in the estate. Each Resident Account is connected to the current house the resident is occupying.

STEP 1

Click the Create Account Button Above or click here

STEP 2

Search and Select the house you are currently residing in and click the request account button

STEP 3

Fill the Account Creation Request Form and Submit.

STEP 4

An Admin Verifies the request and approves the request.

  • Your Default Username is the first 4 letters of your Last Name and the first 3 of your first name
  • Example: Adebisi Michael = AdebMic
